Goff breaks NFL record as Lions beat Seahawks

Seattle Seahawks were beaten for the first time this season as Jared Goff starred in a 42-29 victory for the Detroit Lions. Goff completed all 18 of his pass attempts, the most in an NFL game without an incompletion, including two for touchdowns. The quarterback also scored his first career receiving touchdown. "It's good when the ball doesn't hit the ground," Goff told ESPN.
